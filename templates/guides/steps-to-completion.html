1. Pretest in the Following Content Areas
    1. Language Arts/Reading
    2. Math
    3. Science
    4. Social Studies
  2. Review Pretest Scores
    1. If you score under 160, study the curriculum.
    2. If you score over 160, schedule an official test.
  3. Use GGV Curriculum to Study
  4. When Modules in the Curriculum Are Completed, Request a Post Test
  5. Review Post Test Scores
    1. If you score under 149, study the curriculum.
    2. If you score over 149, schedule an official test(s).
  6. Review Official Test Score(s)
    1. If you score under 145, study the curriculum.
    2. If you score over 145, you passed that test.
  7. Review Pretest Scores If You Have Not Passed 4 Official Tests
    1. If you score under 160, study the curriculum.
    2. If you score over 160, schedule next official test.
  8. Pass all 4 of Your Official GED tests
  9. Graduate
  10. Order Your GED Certificate
